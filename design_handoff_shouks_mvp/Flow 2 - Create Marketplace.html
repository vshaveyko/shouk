Listing rules· optional

Enable auctions

Members can list items as auctions alongside fixed-price listings. Buyers bid; the highest bid at close wins.

Anti-snipe protection

When a bid is placed in the last 2 minutes, the auction extends by 2 minutes. Prevents last-second sniping and gives legitimate bidders a fair chance to respond.

Reserve prices

Allow sellers to set a hidden minimum. If bids don't reach it, the auction ends without a sale.

How does the marketplace sustain itself?

Optional. Skip if you're launching as a free community — you can always turn this on later.
If you charge a fee, all paying members have identical access. Shouks doesn't support permission tiers in v1 — a "pro member" toggle would muddy the community model.

Charge a membership fee

One flat fee for everyone. Collected directly by Shouks's payment processor and deposited to your connected payout account.
