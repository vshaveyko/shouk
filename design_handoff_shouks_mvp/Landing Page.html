How it works

Simple enough to launch today.

Whether you're joining someone else's community or starting your own, the path is short.

01

Create an account

Sign up once with your email or Google. Verify your identity with the accounts you already use — Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, or phone.

02

Join, or create

Apply to marketplaces that match what you collect, trade, or follow. Or define your own — name it, brand it, decide who gets in.

03

Start transacting

List items using the community's form. Bid on auctions. Contact sellers directly. Deals happen between members — Shouks stays out of the way.

Trust lives at the door

Every member gets the same vetting. That's what makes the listings worth something.

Anonymous marketplaces fill up with scams. Curated ones fill up with deals. On Shouks, every member — buyer or seller — clears the same bar before they're let in. No bots, no burner accounts, no guessing who's real.
