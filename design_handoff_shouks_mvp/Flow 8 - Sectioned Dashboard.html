Notes
Sectioned dashboard — final. Sidebar groups by role: Listings (selling, sub-tabs Active/Drafts/Sold), ISO (sourcing, sub-tabs Open/Matches/Closed), Alerts (monitoring, sub-tabs Active/Matches), and Purchases (flat — record of completed buys, pinned to bottom of sidebar with a divider since it has no sub-tabs).
Empty states — please respect these in build:
  • Listings · Active — "No live listings. Create your first listing →" Show recent drafts (if any) below CTA.
  • Listings · Drafts — "No drafts. Drafts auto-save here as you build a listing."
  • Listings · Sold — "Nothing sold yet. Your sales history will live here."
  • Purchases — "No purchases yet. Once you buy something, it shows up here with the receipt and seller record." (No filter chips when empty.)
  • ISO · Open — "No active wanted ads. Post a wanted →" Brief one-liner explaining ISO is public to sellers in that marketplace.
  • ISO · Matches — "No matches yet. We'll notify you when a seller responds." Show this even when Open has 0.
  • ISO · Closed — already handled in mock: "No closed wanted ads yet. Closed ads stay here for your records." (See dashed-border treatment.)
  • Alerts · Active — "No saved searches. Create an alert →" Mention alerts run in the background and can notify instant or daily.
  • Alerts · Matches — "No new matches. New listings that match your saved searches will appear here." Group by alert when matches exist (see mock for grouping pattern).
  • Detail pane (no row selected) — already handled: small icon + "Select a row to see its full detail here." Use this whenever the active sub-tab has zero rows OR the user hasn't clicked one.
Privacy banners are intentional — public sub-tabs (Listings · Active/Sold, ISO · Open) get the eye/info icon line; private sub-tabs (Drafts, Purchases, Alerts · Active) get the lock icon line. Keep these in empty states too — they help orient users.
Counts in the sidebar reflect total items across all sub-tabs of that section. New-dot indicator only appears if there are unread/new items in any sub-tab.
