Context

Seven things to know

Grouped by day. Unread items get a blue rail + softly tinted row, so the eye can count at a glance without reading. The bell's red "7" goes away the moment the tray opens (not when each is read).

NotesEach notification = 1 icon (typed by event), 1 sentence of plain English, marketplace tag + relative time, and an optional inline CTA. Tabs filter by category — Sales (offers/bids/auction/sold), ISO (matches), Mentions (messages/reviews). Clicking the row marks just that one read. Mark all read wipes the batch. Across-marketplace activity lives in the same tray — the mp-tag tells you where each thing happened.

Read state

After "Mark all read" the blue rails disappear and the bell goes quiet. History stays — notifications are not dismissed, just de-emphasized.

NotesRead state is just the absence of emphasis — same rows, same content, same order. We don't hide or collapse. Users should be able to re-open the tray and retrace their steps.
